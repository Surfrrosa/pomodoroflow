Privacy Policy

PomodoroFlow is built with radical simplicity and privacy in mind.

  • No account required.
  • No personal data collected beyond what's strictly necessary for core functionality.
  • Notifications are used only to help you transition between focus and break sessions.
  • Analytics: We use Google Analytics to understand how users interact with our app and website. This includes anonymized usage data to help us improve the experience. No personally identifiable information is collected.
